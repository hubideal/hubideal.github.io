Jordan's Refugees & Populations of Concern

The Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan (population 9.7 million) serves an important role for the international community by receiving high numbers of refugees from the region. The visualization below allows the user to explore both the population size and percentages of these populations (horizontal axis) for the eleven countries with the most migration from 1991-2016. Clicking on the legend boxes and the checkbox below allows the user to change the country and population vs. percentage views. Data to complete this visualization was obtained from the Humanitarian Data Exchange.
