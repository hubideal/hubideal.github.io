Health Needs and Priorities of Syrian Refugees Living in Jordan
Perspectives from Refugees and Health Care Providers

This visualization summarizes qualitative research from the article "Health needs and priorities of Syrian refugees in camps and urban settings in Jordan: perspectives of refugees and health care providers" (Al-Rousan, Schwabkey, Jirmanus, & Nelson, 2018). The study was completed in 2017, before changes were made to the healthcare subsidy for Syrian refugees. The study provides perspectives of health needs from three different groups: service providers, Syrian refugees living in urban areas, and Syrian refugees living in camps.
