What's is a concept map


This concept map visualizes the key messages from focus groups. The black bars in the center are short tiles of key concepts. The words that circle around the middle row of titles (black bars) are high frequency words that were found from the corpus (report). When the user clicks on a bar or circle it leads to a new webpage with a key message (theme) or high frequency word in the center. Around the circle are the words and key concepts that are related to the center word or concept. When the user clicks on a key message, they are then taken to a website with a description of the key message and sentences found from the key message.


If the user wants to return to the original homepage, the user can click on the Full Data link
