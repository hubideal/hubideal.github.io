What's is a concept map


This concept map visualizes the key messages from over a dozen focus groups of urban Syrian refugees living in Jordan. The black bars in the center are short tiles of key concepts. The words that circle around the middle column of titles (black bars) are high frequency words that were found from the corpus (report). When the user clicks on a bar or circle it leads to a new webpage with a key message (theme) or high frequency word in the center surrounded by words that are related to the text.
