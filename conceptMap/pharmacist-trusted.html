Pharmacists as Trusted Providers


Description: Syrian refugees from the focus group discussed the importance of pharmacies as a healthcare service. Several participants discussed that pharmacists became trusted healthcare providers for refugees. They were perceived as a higher quality and lower cost option in certain circumstances.

Comments:

  • 'The participants stressed that there is no free health service center makes them feel safe. Instead, they prefer to go to a nearby or trusted pharmacist.'

  • 'They always help family members and friends in any medical condition by taking them to this trusted pharmacist.'

  • 'She took the child to a well-known pharmacist in the area where she lives who diagnosed the condition and gave her a different drug. The child’s health improved because of this new drug, which deepened the family’s trust in this pharmacist. The pharmacist was kind and sympathetic and showed compassion with the child’s condition.'
