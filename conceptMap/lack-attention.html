Lack of Attention


Description: In addition to their concern about quality at free clinics, participants also expressed concern about the lack of attention at clinics from healthcare providers

Comments:

  • 'Carelessness by employees.'

  • 'They stressed that doctors show no interest in the patient's health condition, unless the service center is not free.'

  • 'After arriving at the center, they enter and endure bad reception. They stand in line to register their names, and then go to accounting to pay for the diagnosis. Afterwards, they wait until their turn comes and then wait in hours and after all this trouble, they see the doctor, who without any examinations, talks to them and provides the diagnosis.'
